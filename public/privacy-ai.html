Privacy & AI Notice

What data we collect

We collect only what is necessary for learning: your progress, practice sessions, vocabulary cards, and skill confidence scores. We do not store unnecessary personal data such as exact date of birth.

Why we use AI

Knuut uses AI to provide learning suggestions and practice feedback. AI generates recommendations — final decisions are always made by the teacher. You can request that a teacher review any AI suggestion before it affects your records.

Teacher in control

All AI outputs require human validation. Teachers can override any AI suggestion. The platform is designed so that AI assists; it does not make autonomous decisions about your learning.

Where your data is stored

Your data is stored in the European Union (EU). We use Neon PostgreSQL hosted in EU regions to ensure compliance with GDPR and EU data protection laws.

Your rights

  • Export your data: Download a copy of your progress, sessions, and scores.
  • Delete your data: Request full deletion of your account and all associated data.

Use the "Download My Data" or "Delete My Data" options in your dashboard.

Explainability

When AI suggests a focus topic or practice area, we explain why. For example: "Based on repeated errors in past tense usage (3 sessions)". We avoid black-box decisions.
